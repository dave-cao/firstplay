About Us

First Play is a grassroots organisation started by students at the University of Alberta.

Our goal is to reduce the barriers to physical activity and wellness programs that many indigenous youth and their families experience all across our communities

Mission Statement

To make an impact and a well thought out plan to break the barriers to physical activity in the indigenous community
